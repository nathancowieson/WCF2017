Our leadership team

John Green (Community Minister)

The newest member of our eldership team is our Community Minister, Rev John R Green. After a 28-year career in radio and television management, John entered full-time pastoral work in 2008, for which he was originally trained. Before coming to WCF, John served as Senior Minister for 10 years with a church just outside of London. Besides being a Bible teacher, John is a photographer, author, artist, poet, collector and closet comedian. And, he loves Southern fried chicken and good coffee too!
