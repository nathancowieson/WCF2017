Balsam Family Project

Balsam Family Project is a local independent charitable organisation that supports families with education programs and practical care. While there are no formal links between Balsam and WCF several members of our church family are enthusiastic volunteers and vocal supporters of the charity. In this capacity Balsam is close to our heart.

For more information speak to Andy at WCF or visit the Balsam web site.
